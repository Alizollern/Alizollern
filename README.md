I am a Systems-focused Software Engineer who treats distributed architectures not just as code, but as **high-performance engineering art**. Over the last 4+ years, I've specialized in tearing down legacy monoliths and architecting fault-tolerant, event-driven microservices that handle massive scale.

- 🧠 **Current Obsession:** Orchestrating complex business workflows using  `Rust`, `Go`, and `Java` at **Kaspi.kz**.
